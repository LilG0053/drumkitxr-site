Drum Simulator XR

Welcome to Drum Simulator XR! This VR drumkit allows users to practice drums without a real drumset!
Click the 'A' button to hit the kick drum, and press the '+' or '-' buttons on the panel to raise or lower the set.
Supports both AR and VR. Hand tracking is technically supported but the kick drum is not supported yet.
Press 'switch' to switch the background scene. Please reach out to me with any suggestions/feedback!
